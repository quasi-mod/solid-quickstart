Speculation Rules Prerender Demo

This page has a speculation rule defined in its <head>. As soon as this page loads, it instructs the browser to begin prerendering the target page in the background.

When you click the link below, the navigation should be almost instantaneous because the page has already been loaded and rendered.

Click here to navigate to the prerendered page

How to Verify it's Working

  1. Open this page in Google Chrome.
  2. Right-click anywhere and select "Inspect" to open DevTools.
  3. Go to the Application panel.
  4. On the left menu, scroll down to Background Services and click on Speculative loads.
  5. You should see a row for https://quasimodjsplayground.netlify.app/ with the "Action" as Prerender and the "Status" as Success.
